A New Vision for Earth Science

EMIT was made to improve scientists' understanding of the role of mineral dust in the Earth's climate system. Mineral dust plays a role in everything from cloud formation to radiative forcing, but scientists had a limited understanding of its composition and distribution. EMIT is providing scientists with the data they need to better understand the role of mineral dust in the climate system and to develop more accurate climate models.

Exploring the Earth's Mineral Dust

Science: NASA's EMIT mission is mapping the mineral composition of arid dust source regions around the world. This data will help scientists to better understand the role of mineral dust in the Earth's climate system and to develop more accurate climate models.

Keeping you one step ahead of the storm

EMIT data can be used to track the movement of dust storms and to identify areas that are at risk of being impacted. This information can be used to develop early warning systems for dust storms and to inform evacuation and disaster response efforts.
