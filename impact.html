By providing data on the composition of Earth's surface, including minerals and other materials, EMIT contributes to the development of more accurate climate models. EMMISSIONS
EMIT is capable of detecting and monitoring greenhouse gas emissions such as methane and CO2. This capability is vital for tracking the sources and trends of these gases, which play a critical role in climate change. Identifying and addressing greenhouse gas emissions are essential steps in mitigating climate change.
